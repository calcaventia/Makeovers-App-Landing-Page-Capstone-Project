LuxeLodgings

Introducing our app feature, LuxeLodgings, that revolutionizes the way people find inspiration for their home design projects. Book an apartment or room that has unique design and features built by contractors we trust. Design your future home and enjoy a staycation with us. With this app, designing a dream living space has never been easier.

  • Clients can choose to stay in these spaces for a short period, experiencing firsthand what living in their new space would be like.
  • This experience allows them to choose which design features they would like to incorporate in their own living spaces.
